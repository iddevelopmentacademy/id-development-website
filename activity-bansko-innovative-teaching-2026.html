About the Training

ID Development Academy hosted a group of seven school educators from North Macedonia for a week-long Erasmus+ professional development course focused on Innovative Teaching Methods in School. The training took place in Bansko — one of Bulgaria's most vibrant towns, combining a rich cultural heritage with a modern international atmosphere.

The programme was designed to equip participants with practical, ready-to-use tools and strategies for transforming everyday classroom practice. Through a mix of workshops, group activities and reflective sessions, teachers explored modern pedagogical approaches that are directly applicable across different school subjects and age groups.

Programme Highlights

  • Active learning strategies and student-centred classroom design
  • Gamification and interactive digital tools for lesson delivery
  • Project-based learning — from planning to assessment
  • Flipped classroom methodology and blended learning models
  • Collaborative cross-curricular approaches and team teaching
  • Formative assessment techniques and peer feedback frameworks
  • Cultural exchange and best-practice sharing among European educators

All 7 participants successfully completed the programme and received an official ID Development Academy certificate of participation, co-branded with the Erasmus+ programme logo.

"The training in Bansko gave us not just new teaching tools, but also the confidence to bring real change into our classrooms. The organisation was excellent and the learning environment was truly inspiring."

— Participant, North Macedonia

Location — Bansko, Bulgaria

Bansko is a UNESCO-recognised historic town nestled at the foot of the Pirin Mountains in southwestern Bulgaria. Known for its traditional stone architecture, vibrant culture and proximity to nature, it provides an exceptional setting for international learning exchanges — combining focused professional development with memorable cultural experiences.

Participants had the opportunity to explore the old town, experience authentic Bulgarian hospitality, and connect with colleagues from the Erasmus+ network in an inspiring environment far from the routine of everyday school life.
