ID Development Academy — how and why we use cookies.

Last updated: May 2025

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site function correctly and, where applicable, allow the site owner to understand how visitors use the site.

2. Who We Are

This Cookie Policy applies to the website of ID Development Academy, operated at id-development.netlify.app. For any questions, contact us at id.development@yahoo.com.

3. Cookies We Use

Our website is an informational site with a contact form. We use only strictly necessary cookies required to operate the site:

Cookie Provider Purpose Duration Consent required
__cf_bm Netlify / Cloudflare Bot protection and site security 30 minutes No
Session cookie Netlify Enables contact form submission Session No

We do not use advertising, tracking, analytics, or social media cookies. If this changes, this policy will be updated and your consent will be requested before any non-essential cookies are set.

4. Legal Basis

The cookies listed above are strictly necessary for the operation of this website. Under Article 5(3) of the ePrivacy Directive (2002/58/EC) as implemented in EU member states, and the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) Regulation (EU) 2016/679, strictly necessary cookies are exempt from the prior consent requirement.

The legal basis for processing any personal data related to these cookies is our legitimate interest (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR) in maintaining a secure and functional website.

5. Your Rights and Browser Controls

Because we only use strictly necessary cookies, there is currently no opt-in mechanism required. You can still control and delete cookies through your browser settings at any time:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of this website, including the contact form.
