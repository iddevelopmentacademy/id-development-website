Erasmus+ orientation

Erasmus+ course delivery with a strong practical focus

ID Development Academy offers course formats that are well suited to international learning mobility. The content is organised for participants who need a clear structure, useful methodologies, visible outcomes and a training experience that can be transferred to their own institution after returning home.

Relevant for Erasmus+ groups

  • School education staff mobility participants
  • Adult learning and NGO teams
  • Groups interested in pedagogy, digital skills and innovation
  • Institutions requesting custom 5-day or 7-day formats
What participants receive

More than a presentation-based course

The programmes are built to support meaningful learning mobility rather than passive attendance. Participants work with tools, cases, design tasks, peer exchange and concrete implementation ideas connected to their own practice.

01

The programme can be adapted to the group profile, requested topic and intended learning priorities.

03

04

After the mobility

The course design supports dissemination, transfer and internal use of learning in the participant institution.
