This Privacy Policy is drafted in compliance with:

3. What Personal Data We Collect

We collect personal data only when you actively provide it to us through the contact form on this website. The data collected includes:

  • Name — provided by you in the contact form
  • Email address — provided by you in the contact form
  • Message content — the text you write in the contact form

We do not collect sensitive personal data (special categories under Article 9 GDPR), financial data, or data relating to children.

We do not use analytics tools, advertising pixels, or tracking technologies. The only technical data processed automatically is standard server log data (IP address, browser type, page visited, timestamp) handled by our hosting provider Netlify as part of their infrastructure.

5. How Long We Keep Your Data

Contact form submissions are retained only for as long as necessary to handle your enquiry and any follow-up communication — typically no longer than 12 months from the last interaction. After this period, data is deleted. Server log data held by Netlify is subject to Netlify's own retention policy.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We share data only with the following processor in order to operate this website:

Your data is not transferred to any other third parties.

Netlify relies on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission as the legal mechanism for such transfers, in accordance with Article 46 GDPR.

8. Your Rights Under GDPR

As a data subject under EU law, you have the following rights:

Right of access

Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you. (Art. 15 GDPR)

Right to rectification

Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data. (Art. 16 GDPR)

Right to erasure

Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten"). (Art. 17 GDPR)

Right to restriction

Ask us to limit the processing of your data in certain circumstances. (Art. 18 GDPR)

Right to data portability

Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format. (Art. 20 GDPR)

Right to object

Object to processing based on legitimate interests. (Art. 21 GDPR)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at id.development@yahoo.com. We will respond within 30 days as required by GDPR Article 12.
