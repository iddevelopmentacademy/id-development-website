Programme / Sample Agenda

Sample 5-day training agenda for educator mobility.

This sample agenda shows how a course can combine orientation, hands-on workshops, collaborative learning, reflection and certification.

Day 1

Welcome, orientation and learning goals

  • Introduction to participants and institutions
  • Setting expectations and learning objectives
  • Overview of the course theme and methodology
Day 2

Workshops and practical exploration

  • Hands-on training sessions
  • Collaborative group tasks
  • Reflection on classroom or institutional application
Day 3

Deepening practice and peer exchange

  • Examples from different education contexts
  • Peer learning and small-group discussion
  • Design of participant mini-projects or implementation ideas
Day 4

Application, adaptation and local inspiration

  • Planning for transfer to participants’ institutions
  • Interactive tasks and practical materials
  • Cultural and intercultural learning elements
Day 5

Evaluation, follow-up and certification

  • Participant presentations and reflection
  • Next steps for implementation and future cooperation
  • Feedback session and certificate ceremony
